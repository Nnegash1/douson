Wheel Balance

They say balance is the key to a happy life, and if your tires could speak, they’d surely agree. Correctly balanced wheels don’t just keep you driving smoothly; they also reduce noise, vibration, and premature tread wear. If you’re due for a wheel balance, or your handling feels off, stop in and let us check your wheel balance.

Tire Rotation

Sometimes you just need a change of scenery. And sometimes, your tires do, too. If you don’t have them rotated on a routine basis, the tires that absorb the most force can wear prematurely and compromise your handling. Help your tires last longer with a visit to us, especially if you think you’re overdue for a rotation, and let one of our trusted technicians help ensure you’re getting the most from your tire investment.

TIRE PRESSURE MONITERING SYSTEM

Places to go? People to see? Both are more enjoyable without a glowing TPMS—short for tire pressure monitoring system—light distracting you along the way. So, if an illuminated symbol of a tire profile with an exclamation point in the middle appears on your dashboard, indicating that the air inside your tires may be low, or if you think your tire pressure may be low but a light isn’t on, stop by and let one of our certified technicians check it for you.

Tire Installation

When you think about all the places a new set of tires will take you, it’s pretty exciting. So to get the most mileage from your investment and to put the most check marks on your things to see and do list it pays to have them expertly installed. When your technician is one of our trusted professionals you maximize your investment even more

Tire Pressure Check

With so much to do and see, it can be easy to overlook your tire pressure. Under- or over-inflation, however, can adversely affect handling and gas mileage, not to mention the life of your tires. If you think your tire pressure is off, let one of our experts make sure they’re properly inflated so you’re ready for what’s next on your to-do list.

Alignment Check

Most things just work better when they’re in proper alignment, and your tires are no exception. When they’re properly aligned, getting anywhere is smoother, and when they’re not, everything from your steering to tread wear suffers. If you think your wheels may need to be aligned, come in and let one of our technicians check your vehicle for free.

Tire Consulation

There’s a lot to consider when buying tires, and sometimes it’s hard to know which is right for you. That’s what our technicians are for. Our service technicians care about you feeling confident in your tires and vehicle which is why DOUSON Tires offers a free tire consultation. Whether you have already found some tire options, or are brand-new to tire shopping, we’ll help you sort through your options and choose the tire that’s right for you, so you can focus on your next destination.
